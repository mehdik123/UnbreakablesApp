## 🎨 Image Requirements:
- Format: PNG or JFIF
- Style: Anatomical body diagram with highlighted muscle group in RED
- Background: Transparent or solid color
- Size: Recommended 400x400px or larger

## 🔥 What the app does:
The Performance Analytics will automatically:
- Show **RED GLOW** when muscle volume is **INCREASING** (good progress!)
- Show **BLUE GLOW** when muscle volume is **DECREASING** (needs attention)
- Show **GRAY GLOW** when muscle volume is **STABLE**

## 📍 Where to find similar images:
- Search for "muscle anatomy diagram PNG"
- Use icon/illustration sites like Flaticon, Freepik
- Or create custom ones matching the shoulders style
